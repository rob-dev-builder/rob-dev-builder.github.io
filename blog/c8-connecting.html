Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

I have noticed with my friends that being vulneratble does help to build trust. Opening up with friends shows that you are willing to expose yourself and trust that your friend does not take advandage if your vunrebility. My friends tend to feel more relaxed when I am open with them. This in turn allows them to be free to open up and become vunreable (if they wish too). I can see how becoming vunreable to friends can create great trust.

Try empathic listening - how did it go?

I haven't had a chance to try a formal empathic listening session with anyone yet. But, I will try it informally with my friends later this week. I like the idea of starting a response with "This is what I hear" to confirm that I have understood what they have said.

How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

I found this relaxing and it also gave me a better perspective on the other persons point of view. Sometimes it can be hard to remove yourself from a heated discussion. However, this technique is useful in pulling your self away from an argument and remembering that the other person has valid ideas and experiences too.
