How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

I found this topic very interesting. I like the idea of thinking about Success and Failure as emotional experiences. I agree that these experiences can sometimes make us either 'grab hold' or avert depending on the circumstances. For example if I feel bad about failing at something then I might try to avoid doing that thing again because I felt negatively about it. I like the idea that to overcome this - we should learn to be ok with Success or Failure. By being ok with Success or Failure then we can become more resilient because certain situations will not trigger us into feeling bad about failure or not worthy of success.

I enjoyed the meditation exercise. I did struggle at first to think of experiences that would help me in the this exercise. I did become more aware of how my body felt when I am in these situations.
