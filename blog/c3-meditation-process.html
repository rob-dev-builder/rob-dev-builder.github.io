How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I felt like I was regularly reminding myself that I did not need to make the new site perfect. Sometimes I felt like I often and spent too much time styling the new site. However, I also felt that I spent a good amount of time wireframming and planning the site before actually gettting down to the styling. I was glad that I spent some time on making the site responsive and 'mobile first' before starting any of the styling work.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I knew a little bit about meditation before reading chapter two. I knew that focusning on brething was an important aspect of mediation

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I learnt a few practical techniques to help when mediating. For example, keeping your eyes half closed can be useful because it keeps you calm but also alert. I also learnt that people who meditate tend to show more empathy towards other people.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Yes, keeping your eyes half shut sounds like a good way to stay calm and alert. Maintaining a posture that allows you to breathe easily is very important. Also, it is important not to critise yourself if you do have distracting thoughts while you are meditating.
