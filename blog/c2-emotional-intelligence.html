Chapter One of Search Inside Yourself

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

One of the most interesiing things was the list of top six competencies that distinguish high performance in the tech industry. The top two competencies were purley emotional intelligence competences. This does make sense since being able to maintain strong motivation and have empathy for others is crucial when working in small teams.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I think this idea is interesting. I like the idea of taking two minutes everyday just relax and monitor your own thoughts/feelings. I think this can be very benefical when you are in a stressful situtaiton. This can allow you to be removed from the moment and think clearly about what choices you have.
